Ennexus

Ennexus is a technical festival that has been exclusively designed for students who are interested in the field of Computer Science. This platform provides students with the opportunity to showcase their technical skills and compete with their peers from their own institutions and others as well. It is an excellent platform for students to participate and enhance their technical expertise.
